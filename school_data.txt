Twifo Hemang Shalom School was founded in 2004. It started its educational mission to provide high-quality training and deep moral uprightness.
The founders, proprietors, and directors of Twifo Hemang Shalom School are Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu (PhD) and Mrs. Christiana Owusu.
Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu (PhD) is a director and co-founder. He is a native of Kumasi, Ashanti Region, Ghana, an alumnus of Baylor University, and the retired Founding & Senior Pastor of Amazing Grace Baptist Church of Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
Mrs. Christiana Owusu is a co-founder and director of the school who manages campus community care standards, student welfare, and character formation programs.
The administration and management leadership team of the school consists of Mr. Benjamin Quao (Administrator), Mr. Christopher Marfo Addae (Head of Academic Affairs), and Mr. Anthony Obeng Essel (Headmaster).
Twifo Hemang Shalom School opening hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday.
Admissions for the upcoming semester are currently open. Parents and students can apply online by visiting the Admissions tab in the top navigation menu and filling out the application form.
We offer premium, separate boarding facilities and hostel accommodation for both boys and girls under full adult supervision and including daily meals.
The school campus is located in Twifo Hemang, Ghana. Visitors are welcome to tour the campus during official administrative hours.
The school uniform consists of a brown shirt with the school crest logo and khaki trousers or skirts. Uniforms can be purchased at the campus store.
Extracurricular activities include dynamic student clubs, football and sports groups, and a vibrant music and choir department.
