3. FOUNDATION HISTORY, INSTITUTIONAL GROWTH, & AMENITIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Established: September 2004 (Commenced operations with a pioneer enrollment of only four students).
- Current Scale: Over 720 active students spanning Nursery, Primary, and Junior High School (JHS) departments, guided by a professional team of 42 educators and personnel.
- Core Pillars: Academic Excellence, Holistic Social Growth, and Deep Christian Moral Uprightness.
- Boarding & Accommodation: Premium, entirely separate residential boarding facilities and hostel accommodations are provided for both boys and girls. The program runs under full adult supervision and includes nutritious daily meals.
- Official Attire & Uniforms: The school uniform consists of a tan shirt decorated with the official school crest logo, paired with khaki trousers or skirts. Uniform sets are available for purchase directly at the campus store.
- Extracurricular Activities: Includes dynamic student-led academic clubs, competitive football and sports groups, and a highly vibrant music and performance choir department.
