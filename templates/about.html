About Our Institution

Discover the history, leadership, and foundational pillars of Twifo Hemang Shalom School.

OUR HISTORY & MISSION

Founded in September 2004 with a pioneer enrollment of only four (4) students, Twifo Hemang Shalom School was built on a vision to provide a serene, academically rigorous, and deeply values-based educational environment. Over the decades, the school has blossomed into a premier landmark institution within the Central Region of Ghana, currently serving over 720 active students across our Nursery, Primary, and Junior High School (JHS) departments.

Our mission balances elite academic preparation with holistic personal development. We are dedicated to ensuring that every child leaves our halls anchored in Christian moral uprightness, critical thinking habits, and the technical competence required to make a meaningful impact on the global stage.

Founders & Proprietors

Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu (PhD) | Co-Founder & Director

A native of Kumasi, Ashanti Region, Ghana, Dr. Owusu is a distinguished alumnus of Baylor University and the retired Founding & Senior Pastor of Amazing Grace Baptist Church in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. His international administrative background and pastoral dedication provide the spiritual, ethical, and structural anchor of our entire school ecosystem.

Mrs. Christiana Owusu | Co-Founder & Director

Working hand-in-hand with campus leadership, Mrs. Christiana Owusu lovingly directs our institutional community care standards, student welfare parameters, and core character formation structures. Her focus ensures that the residential life experience matches the school's high academic expectations.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are the daily campus operating hours? Official administrative operations and classes run strictly from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Visitors are welcome to schedule campus tours during these times.
Q: What is the boarding house accommodation structure? We offer premium, completely separated residential hostel blocks for boys and girls on campus. Each dormitory stays under full adult supervision by resident houseparents and includes full daily meal rosters.
Q: What uniform profile does the school use? Our official uniform features a tailored tan shirt decorated with the school crest logo, paired neatly with standard khaki trousers or skirts. Complete uniform pieces can be bought directly at the campus store.
