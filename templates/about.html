ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Founded in 2004, Twifo Hemang Shalom School is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, holistic growth, and deep moral uprightness.

Our History & Mission

Twifo Hemang Shalom School was established in 2004 with a clear vision: to provide a nurturing, rigorous, and values-based educational environment for students. Over the decades, we have remained true to our foundational pillars, balancing top-tier academic instruction with character development to ensure our graduates are equipped to lead with integrity.

Proprietors & Directors

Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu (PhD)

Co-Founder & Director

A native of Kumasi, Ashanti Region, Ghana, Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu is an alumnus of Baylor University and the Founding & Senior Pastor (Retired) of the Amazing Grace Baptist Church of Atlanta, GA, USA. His global experience in leadership and pastoral care forms the spiritual and ethical anchor of our institution.

Mrs. Christiana Owusu

Co-Founder & Director

Working hand-in-hand with the structural management team, Mrs. Christiana Owusu guides the community care standards, student welfare, and character programs that define the student experience at Shalom School.
