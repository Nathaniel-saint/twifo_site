Our Governance

Founders & Proprietors

Twifo Hemang Shalom School was intentionally established by Rev. Dr. Robert Yaw Owusu (PhD) and Mrs. Christiana Owusu.

A proud native of Kumasi, Ashanti Region, and an esteemed alumnus of Baylor University, Dr. Owusu served as the Founding & Senior Pastor of Amazing Grace Baptist Church in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, before focusing his efforts on local educational initiatives. Working in lockstep, Mrs. Christiana Owusu oversees core residential care dynamics, student welfare parameters, and holistic character formation tracks.
